Our origins

Our company was founded in 2024 by a small team of enthusiastic euntreprenuers aiming to keep up with the rapidly developing world of virtual reality technology.

The founders additionally wanted to target other tech savvy individuals who would be interested in migrating tasks typically designed for phones to the more immersive world of spacial computing.

We are actively devloping applications for multiple platforms of virtual reality machines.
